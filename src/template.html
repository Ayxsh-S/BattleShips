🚢 Battleship Instructions

Note!!!

When placing ships make sure pointer is above cell or ship will not place

Two player mode can be played by handing over device to next person

🎯 Objective

Sink all of your opponent’s ships before they sink yours!

⚙️ Setup

  1. Each player’s fleet consists of 5 ships:
    • Carrier (5 cells)
    • Battleship (4 cells)
    • Cruiser (3 cells)
    • Submarine (3 cells)
    • Destroyer (2 cells)
  2. Place ships horizontally or vertically (no diagonals).
  3. Ships cannot overlap.

🔫 Gameplay

  1. Players take turns firing by selecting a grid cell.
  2. The result is shown:
    • Hit 💥 = part of a ship was struck.
    • Miss 🌊 = no ship at that cell.
  3. When all parts of a ship are hit, that ship is sunk.

🏆 Winning

The first player to sink all 5 enemy ships wins the game.

💡 Tips

  • Spread your ships out — don’t cluster them.
  • When you score a hit, search nearby cells to finish the ship.
  • Track your opponent’s moves to guess their strategy.
